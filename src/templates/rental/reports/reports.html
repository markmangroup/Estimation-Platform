Revenue Report

Provides a summary of income generated from equipment rentals, including total revenue, segment breakdown.

Stock Report

Provides a detailed overview of equipment inventory, broken down by location/customer location (rented), in transit, and warehouse (available) as of the selected reporting date.

Stock Movement Report

Tracks all movements of equipment, including additions, removals, and transfers between locations.

In Transit Report

Lists equipment currently being transported between locations or to/from customers.

Planned Removal Report

Details scheduled pickups or returns of equipment, including dates and locations.

Planned Deliveries Report

Outlines upcoming equipment deliveries, with delivery dates, destinations, and customer details.

Equipment Utilization Report

Analyzes how often and how long equipment has been rented, providing insights into utilization rates and demand.
